FAQ

Quick answers

Everything you might want to know before installing.

Is LifeMaster AI really free?
Yes. The full app is free, forever. No subscriptions, no ads, no premium tier locking the good features. You can support us with a chai if you want — it's optional.
Do you collect or sell my data?
Never. LifeMaster AI works fully offline. Your goals, journal entries, and progress live only on your device. We have no servers logging your activity. Read our privacy policy for the full breakdown.
Does it work without internet?
Yes — 100% of features work offline. We only use the network to check for app updates and fetch optional content like daily quotes. The app never depends on connectivity.
What permissions does the app need?
Only what's needed: notifications (so reminders work) and optional storage access (so you can export your data). No camera, no contacts, no location. See privacy policy.
How are updates delivered?
Through Google Play. The app checks once a day and prompts you when a new version is available. Critical updates may show a stronger prompt, but you're always in control.
Can I export my data?
Yes. The app includes one-tap export of your journal, goals, and progress as a portable file. Your data is yours — we mean it.
Is there an iOS version?
Not yet. We're focused on Android first to keep quality high. iOS is on our roadmap.
How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Email us at kive.apps@gmail.com or use the contact form. We respond to every message within 48 hours.
