LifeMaster AI

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Kive Apps

The simple truth

We collect nothing about you — ever
The app never connects to the internet
No account or sign-up of any kind
Your data lives only on your phone
No ads. No tracking. No third-party anything.

What do we know about you?

Nothing. Truly. We don't know your name, your age, your location, or how you use the app. There is no registration, no sign-in, and no profile to create. You open the app and it just works — completely anonymously.

Where does your data live?

Everything you add to the app — your goals, habits, daily scores, journal entries, moods, health logs — is saved directly on your phone. It never travels anywhere. It never reaches us. Because we have no servers for it to reach.

Your personal journey stays personal. No one — not us, not any third party — can ever see what you've written or tracked in LifeMaster AI.

Does the app go online?

Never. LifeMaster AI is a fully offline app. It works without Wi-Fi, without mobile data, even in airplane mode. It will never make any kind of internet connection — not when you open it, not in the background, not ever.

Why does the app need certain permissions?

Some features need your phone's permission to work. Here's exactly what each one does:

Notifications

Sends you habit reminders, focus session alerts, and your evening reflection prompts at the times you choose. Everything is created on your phone — nothing is sent anywhere.

Vibration

Gives you a gentle physical nudge when you complete a task or reach a milestone. Nothing is collected.

Restart after reboot

Makes sure your reminders keep working after you restart your phone. No data is involved.

Background timer

Keeps your Focus Timer running while you use other apps, so your session doesn't stop the moment you switch screens.

Screen time insights Optional

If you choose to enable this, the app shows which apps distract you most. This is based entirely on data your phone already has — it never goes anywhere else. You can use the app fully without enabling this.

Focus Shield Optional

A gentle pause that appears when you open a distracting app you've chosen to limit. Completely optional, completely local to your device. Turn it on or off any time from Settings.

What about backups?

The app lets you create a backup of all your data. It's protected with a password that only you know — we can never access it. The backup file is saved on your phone or wherever you choose to store it. It never goes to any cloud service automatically.

Is LifeMaster AI for children?

LifeMaster AI is designed for people aged 13 and above. Some features — like the addiction recovery tools and urge management system — deal with mature, real-life challenges and are not meant for young children.

If this policy changes

We'll update this page with a new date at the top. We'll never introduce data collection without being completely transparent about it.

We'll get back to you within 48 hours.
