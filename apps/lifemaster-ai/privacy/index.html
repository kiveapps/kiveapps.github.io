LifeMaster AI

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Kive Apps

The Short Version

No account or sign-up of any kind
Your personal data lives only on your phone
Internet is used strictly for safe, anonymous app updates (remote config)
No ads. No tracking. No third-party analytics.

What do we know about you?

Nothing. Truly. We don't know your name, your age, your location, or how you use the app. There is no registration, no sign-in, and no profile to create. You open the app and it just works — completely anonymously.

Where does your data live?

Everything you add to the app — your goals, habits, daily scores, journal entries, moods, health logs — is saved directly on your phone's local database. It never travels anywhere. It never reaches us. Because we have no servers to receive your personal data.

Your personal journey stays personal. No one — not us, not any third party — can ever see what you've written or tracked in LifeMaster AI.

Why does the app connect to the internet?

LifeMaster AI uses the internet strictly in a one-way, read-only capacity. The app connects to our secure, encrypted backend (hosted on GitHub Pages) solely to download new configuration files, daily quotes, coaching tips, and emergency kill-switches.

When the app connects to fetch these updates, it sends zero personal data. It only requests a static file. All core features (journaling, habits, timers) work perfectly offline without any connection.

Why does the app need certain permissions?

Some features need your phone's permission to work. Here's exactly what each one does:

Internet & Network State

Used strictly to securely download remote configuration files (like new daily quotes and app settings) without requiring a full app update. No personal data is sent.

Camera Optional

Used only if you choose to take a photo for your journal or profile. Photos are saved locally on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.

Notifications & Exact Alarms

Sends you habit reminders, focus session alerts, and reflection prompts at the times you choose. Everything is triggered locally by your phone.

Vibration & Wake Lock

Gives you a physical nudge when timers end and ensures your alarms ring exactly on time even if the phone is sleeping.

Restart after reboot

Makes sure your reminders keep working after you restart your phone. No data is involved.

Foreground Service (Background Timer)

Keeps your Focus Timer running accurately while you use other apps, showing a persistent notification so you know it's active.

Screen time (Usage Stats) Optional

If you choose to enable the Focus Shield, the app needs to know when you open a distracting app to block it. This data is processed locally and never leaves your device.

Accessibility Service Optional

Used exclusively by the Focus Shield to detect when you launch apps you've asked us to block. We do not observe your screen content or typing.

What about backups?

The app lets you create a backup of all your data. It's protected with a password that only you know — we can never access it. The backup file is saved on your phone or wherever you choose to store it. It never goes to any cloud service automatically.

Is LifeMaster AI for children?

LifeMaster AI is designed for people aged 13 and above. Some features — like the addiction recovery tools and urge management system — deal with mature, real-life challenges and are not meant for young children.

If this policy changes

We'll update this page with a new date at the top. We'll never introduce data collection without being completely transparent about it.
